About Us

At MindWell AI, we are dedicated to harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to help individuals take control of their mental health. Our innovative platform assesses various factors affecting mental wellness and provides an AI-driven risk prediction for mental illness. By analyzing key behavioral and lifestyle inputs, we aim to offer early detection of potential mental health risks, empowering users to make informed decisions and seek timely support for a healthier mind. MindWell AI is here to guide you on the path to better mental well-being.
